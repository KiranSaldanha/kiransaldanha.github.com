Our Weddings

We, at Wedding and Wishes are committed to creating for you, a Wedding Experience that’s perfect! While weddings are special, sacred occasions, they’re also an operational challenge.

- We’re here to take care of that challenge for you and offer you solutions across every wedding service you can imagine

- We partner with passionate, skilled, accomplished providers guaranteed to give you the best service

- We’re sensitive to your taste, budget and requirements and through our consultation, create exciting options for all aspects of the wedding

- We handle turn-key projects as well as individual services

Pricing Model

At Weddings and Wishes we

- Don’t charge percentage rates; we believe in keeping all transactions transparent and passing on to you, any discount we might enjoy from the providers

- Do charge a consultation fee based on the time and effort we invest in the project. We maintain this as a separate account from that of the payments to the vendors

We’d love for you to tell us your wedding story and just sit back and watch the magic unfold!

Wedding Planners

Why you need them?

We’ve all been through the ‘I can do it myself’ phase whether it’s creating a website, designing a home, or planning a birthday. We’ve also observed the marked difference between a professionally executed product and an amateurish one. What’s important is the time and effort taken and whether we optimized on the right aspect instead of hiring a professional for a more effective result.

Why hiring a professional is good for your wedding:

  1. A wedding is a special occasion and you want nothing but the best, executed to perfection
  2. You want to enjoy a hassle-free experience for you and your family knowing that the planning is in the able hands of a professional
  3. You’ve got experienced planners from the wedding business with the best contacts and relationships with providers; looking out for deals they can pass on to you
  4. You’re able to set aside much-needed time for yourself during the hectic last few weeks
  5. Engage professionals who are honest, transparent, cost-effective and don’t charge a percentage of total costs
  6. Be introduced to and get used to being pampered by planners whose sole purpose is your happiness at the wedding
