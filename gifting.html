How we can help you!

Our Gifts

We, at Wedding and Wishes are committed to creating for you, a Gifting Experience that’s perfect! While occasions are special, gifting for them are a challenge.

- We’re here to take care of that for you and offer you solutions across every possible product and service you can imagine

- We partner with established stores and providers guaranteed to give you the best

- We’re sensitive to your taste, budget and requirements and through our consultation, create exciting gift ideas

Pricing Model

At Weddings and Wishes we

- Don’t charge percentage rates; we believe in keeping all transactions transparent and passing on to you, any discount we might enjoy from the providers

- Do charge a consultation fee based on the time and effort we invest in the project. We maintain this as a separate account from that of the payments to the vendors

- We also handle payments to the vendors

- We’d love for you to describe the occasion and the person you’re gifting to and we’ll do the rest!

Gifting Experts

Why you need them?

We’ve all been through the ‘I can do it myself’ phase whether it’s buying a gift, wrapping it, or delivering it. We’ve also observed the marked difference between a professionally executed product and an amateurish one. What’s important is the time and effort taken and whether we optimized on the right aspect instead of hiring a professional for a more effective result.

Why hiring a professional is good for your gifting:

  1. Gifting for special occasions is important and you want the receiver to be ecstatic with what they’ve got
  2. You want to enjoy a hassle-free experience knowing that the gift hunting is in the able hands of a professional
  3. You’ve got experienced gifting experts from the business with the best contacts and relationships with providers; looking out for deals they can pass on to you
  4. You’ve got professional help to scout around for the best gift ideas and get creative with solutions
  5. See the difference when you gift a thoughtful, well presented, beautiful gift to the person you care for
  6. Get reminders every year for the occasion
